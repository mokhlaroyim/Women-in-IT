Teamwork

A group of female students of Inha University in Tashkent (IUT) participated in the launch of the international contest Technovation Challenge in Uzbekistan. The opening ceremony of the contest was held at the Youth Creative Palace in Tashkent. The organizers of the contest are Tashkent city administration, Tashkent city Womenâ€™s Committee, Kamolot Civic Youth Movement, the UN project on promoting electronic government, Inha University in Tashkent, Center UZINFOCOM. Ministry of development of information technologies and communications of Uzbekistan also provides its support to the programme. Mission of Technovation is to inspire and train girls to solve real problems of society with the help of information technologies and entrepreneurship skills. The participation in the contest is free. It is worth to mention that the contest Technovation has been organized in 78 countries of the world since 2010. The programme is supported by Google, Yahoo, Adobe, Intel, Oracle, and Dropbox. Since 2015, the UN and UN Women have been participating in the organization of the contest. The contest is open for girls in the age from 15 to 18 and aimed at developing critical thinking, entrepreneurship and coding skills. The best team from Uzbekistan will receive valuable prizes and a chance to travel to San Francisco. During the opening ceremony, the girls received detailed information about the contest and its main stages, as well as divided into teams.
