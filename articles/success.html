Success

Students of Inha University in Tashkent (IUT) became winners in several nominations of the contest on developing applications using open government data – MIT.UZ Open Data Challenge 2016. MIT.UZ Open Data Challenge 2016 took place at Inha University in Tashkent on 24-27 November 2016. Over 130 people participated in the hackathon. The contest was organized by the Ministry for Development of Information Technologies and Communications of the Republic of Uzbekistan, UNDP Uzbekistan, «Electronic Government» System Development Center, Public Youth Movement "Kamolot", Inha University in Tashkent and UZINFOCOM Center. Center of Programmist BePro was the partner of hackathon. MIT.UZ Open Data Challenge was organized to popularize idea of open data in Uzbekistan and attract attention of developers and public to open data. The participants of the contest should develop socially important and innovative applications, bots and infographics based on open data, published at open data portal and official website of the state bodies.
